Help & faqs

How can we help you today?

What is Pythoplankton Identifier-AI (Photopytho)?

Pythoplankton Identifier-AI is an AI-powered web-based platform designed to automatically identify phytoplankton. Using the YOLOv8 deep learning algorithm, we provide high-accuracy species recognition capabilities for phytoplankton.

How does Pythoplankton Identifier-AI work?

Pythoplankton Identifier-AI uses advanced deep learning techniques to analyze images of phytoplankton. The algorithm processes the visual features and matches them against a trained database of phytoplankton species. The system then generates a species identification based on the best match.

What types of images does Pythoplankton Identifier-AI support?

Pythoplankton Identifier-AI supports various image formats, such as JPEG, PNG, and TIFF. It is recommended to provide clear and well-focused images for better identification accuracy.

Is Pythoplankton Identifier-AI free to use?

Yes, Pythoplankton Identifier-AI is available for free. Users can upload their images and receive automated species identification results without any cost.

Can Pythoplankton Identifier-AI identify multiple species in a single image?

Yes, Pythoplankton Identifier-AI is capable of detecting and identifying multiple species within a single image. It provides a comprehensive analysis of the species present in the sample.

How accurate is Pythoplankton Identifier-AI in species identification?

Pythoplankton Identifier-AI strives to provide highly accurate species identification. However, it is important to note that the accuracy may vary depending on image quality, species complexity, and database coverage.

Can I contribute my own phytoplankton images to improve the database?

Currently, Pythoplankton Identifier-AI uses a pre-trained database. However, we appreciate the interest in contributing images. You can contact us to discuss potential collaborations or data contributions.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we prioritize the security and privacy of user data. We adhere to strict data protection protocols and do not share or sell personal information to third parties without consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How can I get further assistance or support?

If you have any additional questions, concerns, or need technical support, you can reach out to our support team through the "Contact Us" page. We will be more than happy to assist you.
